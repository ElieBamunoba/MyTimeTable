Privacy Policy for MyTimeTable App

Effective Date: 26th July, 2023

Our Commitment to Privacy

MyTimeTable is a mobile application designed to provide Daystar University students with easy access to their exam timetables. We are committed to respecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your information.

No Personal Information Collection

MyTimeTable does not collect any personal information from its users. We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal details to use the app.

No Data Sharing

We do not share any user data with third parties. Your exam timetable and any information you access through the app are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers or any external services.

No Advertising or Tracking

MyTimeTable does not display advertisements, nor does it use any tracking technologies to monitor user behavior.

Data Security

We take data security seriously. Your exam timetable data is stored locally on your device and is not accessible to anyone else. We implement standard security measures to protect the app's integrity and ensure it operates safely.

Children's Privacy

MyTimeTable is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not collect any personal information from children.

Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect any changes in the app's functionality or to comply with legal requirements. Any updates will be effective when published within the app.
